Hello! 안녕하세요! I am **Jaeung Lee**, a Research Engineer at the AI Center of [Samsung Research](https://research.samsung.com).

My research interests lie in **visualization for machine learning**, with a particular focus on how interactive interfaces can support model evaluation and facilitate human-centered decision-making.

Before joining Samsung Research, I received my M.S. and B.S. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from [Sungkyunkwan University](https://www.skku.edu/eng/) in 2026 and 2024, respectively. During my master's program, I worked at the [Interactive Data Computing Lab](https://idclab.skku.edu) under the supervision of Prof. Jaemin Jo.
