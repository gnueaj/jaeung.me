## Project Overview

PediaViz is a multimodal visualization system for exploring interactions among
prescriptions, clinical tests, longitudinal measurements, and free-text medical
records in pediatric chronic care. It helps clinicians assess a patient's condition
and make decisions within limited consultation time while reducing cognitive load.
