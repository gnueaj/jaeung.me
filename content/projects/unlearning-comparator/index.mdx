## Abstract

Machine unlearning seeks to remove the influence of selected training data from a
trained model. Unlearning Comparator is a visual analytics system for systematically
evaluating the accuracy, efficiency, and privacy of machine unlearning methods. It
supports comparison between two models at the class, instance, and layer levels and
helps users inspect behavioral changes after unlearning. The system also simulates
membership inference attacks to assess whether specific samples may still be
identified as part of the original training set.
