## Abstract

The Unlearning Depth Score (UDS) quantifies the mechanistic depth of LLM unlearning
through activation patching. It first uses a retain-model baseline to identify layers
that encode the target knowledge, then measures how much of that knowledge has been
erased from the unlearned model on a zero-to-one scale. A meta-evaluation spanning 20
metrics, 150 unlearned models, and eight methods evaluates its faithfulness and
robustness, while layer-level case studies reveal how the depth of erasure can vary
across examples.
